Interesting facts about chameleon

  • ALMOST HALF OF ALL KNOWN SPECIES LIVE IN MADAGASCAR.
  • THEY MAINLY CHANGE COLOR IN ORDER TO COMMUNICATE OR REGULATE BODY TEMPERATURE.
  • SKIN CRYSTALS ENABLE THEM TO CHANGE COLOR AT WILL.

Interesting facts about pigeons

  1. Pigeons are highly sociable animals. They will often be seen in flocks of 20-30 birds.
  2. Despite the social perception as dirty and disease-ridden, pigeons are actually very clean animals and there is very little evidence to suggest that they are significant transmitters of disease.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities. They can detect sounds at far lower frequencies than humans are able to, and can thus hear distant storms and volcanoes.

Interesting facts about camel

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
